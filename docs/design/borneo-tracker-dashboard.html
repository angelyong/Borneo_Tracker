Every number on this page rides a five-stage pipeline. The stages are also the business model: measurement is what makes real-world wealth trustworthy — and, in time, ownable by the communities it belongs to.

The business logic: whoever holds the trusted measure of a place's true wealth holds the basis for valuing — and one day tokenising — the real assets behind it, with the yield returning to the community. Borneo Tracker starts at the measure. That's the seat at the table; the rest of the stack is the road ahead.
